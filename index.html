PJS 198: Senior Honors Thesis: Shredding: An Exploration of Preteenhood, Yung Love, and Shredding

Kyle Allen & Sophia Carkonen

Devyn Andrews, Mary Midge Cross, Kelsey McEvoy, Jem Wilner, Sarah Mahoney, Cam Simko, Mel Goldberg, Lydia Schulz, Jack Fritzinger, Annie Miller, Max Bennett, Emery Reifsnyder, Josh Berl, Luca Guadagno, Noah Schwartz, Ballard Glenn Blair, Pilar Bancalari, Tyler Shapiro, Brette Lennon, Maia Tarnas, Ben Thorne, Leah Stern
Lil’ Soph and Special K met as TDC partners in the legendary performance of "Rodehead: An Ode to Lana del Rey" in the Spring of 2014, bonding over humorous differences in height and a shared obsession with early Nickelodeon classics. When they decided to come full circle for the final dance performance of their storied careers by teaming up for a once-in-a-lifetime creative project, the obvious conduit was the groundbreaking early 2000s dramedy "Rocket Power." Rocket Power powerfully explores complex themes like adolescence, gender politics, the geographical superiority of the Western United States, getting into situations and just shredding, man. The project dives into these themes and more, including, but not limited to, shredding, pre-teen love, growing up, and catching the perfect wave in the ocean and in life. In the wise words of Uncle Tito, "Sometimes a pineapple wishes it was a coconut." We would like to thank our wonderful shredders for a fantastical semester of shredding. Our bonds are too strong to be shredded by mere distance. We found pax, lux, et shredding. The Tufts community sorely under-appreciates the art of shredding and we would like to fix that by putting a light on the hill. Later, tennis.

Local art critics a.k.a. random people wearing flannel in Allston have described the experience that Benya and Eric present here as “a TDC dance”, but we like to think we are exploring our own internal struggles with having to simultaneously deal with the onset of puberty and braces (SOS I have a crush on my dentist!!!!!!) using our hot bodz as the medium.
